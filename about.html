Welcome to 2903 Entertainment services based in Ibadan, Nigeria with connects in the US and UK.
We are an all round entertainment outfit from running a record label, Artiste Management, Event Organising and Management and general entertainment business developerment and consultancy. We organise and help in staging memorable parties, concerts, shows and other social events.
2903 Entertainment specializes in hip hop music promotion. Rapper, "Jibsai" (Oladoja Ajibola Mark) founded the outfit as an entertainment group in 2009 as Rapatainer Factory, a platform used for many years to record over 15 studio songs.
Since the Re formation, 2903 have invested in talent hunt and staging shows amongst which are "RAP FEST" an interactive rap music live show at venues in Ibadan, Oyo, Lagos and other cities on tour. Part of the business venture includes fashion with the production of clothings customised and sold exclusively by the company and her partners
