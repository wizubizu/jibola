ABOUT

Jibsai

My name is Ajibola mark-Oladoja but, I am popularly called “Jibsai” a nickname I have had since year 2000. My alter ego is “D’Rapatainer” or simply “Rapatainer” I gave myself that because I Rap to Entertain. I am an Hip-pop performing artist, Event Host, Lyricist, Entertainment business developer.

I founded Rapatainer Factory, an entertainment group in 2009 a platform I used in the last 8 years to record over 15 songs in studio, I performed on several stages and I have met numerous other talented artists and I hope to continue to grow and share what I love with the whole world.

2903 Studios is my new project and official home. It is here to replace Rapatainer Factory. Here, I host an interactive live show “Rap Fest” at venues in Ibadan, Oyo, Lagos and other cities I get the opportunity. I also upload D’Rapatainer videos on my YouTube channel and Facebook page weekly where I gives verse to beats I lay my hands on.

2903 Studios is based in Ibadan, Nigeria. we handle Music production, event management, promotions, photography, graphic designs and media marketing services.

Contact us to manage your musical and technical needs at your occasions, we offer equipment, live music, MC/Host, event ushering and security, get quotations for our services by filling out the form in our Contact page.
